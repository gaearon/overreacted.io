# Video Files to Add to BrainLoop Article

To complete the video integration, you need to add these video files to this directory:

## Required Video Files:

1. **brainloop-demo.mp4** (Main platform demo)
   - Size: Recommended under 50MB
   - Duration: 2-5 minutes
   - Content: Show the main BrainLoop interface, spaced repetition features, and user experience
   - Format: MP4 with H.264 codec

2. **video-learning-demo.mp4** (Video learning integration)
   - Size: Recommended under 30MB  
   - Duration: 1-3 minutes
   - Content: Demonstrate how video content integrates with spaced repetition
   - Format: MP4 with H.264 codec

3. **brainloop-poster.jpg** (Poster image for main demo)
   - Size: Recommended under 2MB
   - Dimensions: 1280x720 or 1920x1080
   - Content: Screenshot or thumbnail from the main demo video
   - Format: JPG or PNG

## Video Optimization Tips:

- Use a video compression tool like HandBrake or FFmpeg
- Target bitrate: 2-5 Mbps for 1080p, 1-2 Mbps for 720p
- Audio: AAC codec, 128-192 kbps
- Frame rate: 24-30 fps
- Resolution: 720p or 1080p

## Example FFmpeg command for optimization:

```bash
ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -c:v libx264 -crf 23 -preset medium -c:a aac -b:a 128k -movflags +faststart output.mp4
```
